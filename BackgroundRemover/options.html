BG Remover

v1.0.0  •  No API key required

How it works

Background removal runs entirely in your browser using the @imgly/background-removal library. An AI model is downloaded from img.ly's CDN on first use (~50 MB) and cached automatically. Your images never leave your device.

Usage

Click the extension icon to open the popup, then:

  • Press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V) to paste an image from your clipboard
  • Or drag & drop an image file onto the drop zone

The background will be removed automatically. Click Download PNG to save the result.

Privacy

Images are processed locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. The AI model files are fetched from staticimgly.com once and cached.

Supported formats

JPEG, PNG, WebP, and most common image formats.
